For Clients

Instead of guessing the cost of a 120 sqm apartment, clients can build their own estimate.

The client enters their details, unit area, and preferred finishing specifications, then receives a detailed printable estimate with clear line items and material quantities.

Client portal

A detailed estimate based on area and selected specifications, ready to print or share with the right company for inspection or contracting.

  • Client and unit details first.
  • Selected finishing specifications.
  • Detailed result with clear items and materials.

Company selection

Clients can browse finishing companies, portfolios, contact data, and social pages, then call or send WhatsApp directly for inspection or contracting.

  • Carefully selected company list.
  • Direct call or WhatsApp contact.
  • A choice built on visible information.

For Finishing Companies

Manage the engineering business from first lead to financial reporting.

Estimates and proposals

Create an estimate with the client during inspection in minutes, then prepare a printable or shareable proposal.

Projects and clients

Organize projects, client records, portfolios, invoices, and design files in one structured workspace.

Financial tracking

Track client payments, expenses, custody items, supervision percentages, and project settlements.

Team and attendance

Manage attendance, leave, salaries, and engineer location checks within the project site range.

Alerts and dashboards

Receive budget alerts and view concise financial dashboards for company and project status.

Existing account import

Move organized Excel data into the app, then extract reports, profit views, and financial positions.
